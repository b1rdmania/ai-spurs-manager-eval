Unified Final Scoring Methodology

Peer Analysis (40% Weight)

  • • Tactical Style: Pressing metrics and front-foot approach
  • • Attacking Potency: xG creation and offensive efficiency
  • • Defensive Solidity: Injury management and availability
  • • Big Game Performance: Record vs top opposition
  • • Youth Development: U23 minutes and academy debuts
  • • Squad Management: Player availability and fitness

Spurs-Fit Model (60% Weight)

Fit Index (60% of Spurs-Fit)
  • • Front-Foot Play: PPDA ≤11, npxGD ≥0.10, xG/shot ≥0.11
  • • Youth Development: U23 minutes ≥15%, academy debuts ≥3
  • • Talent Inflation: Squad value growth vs net spend efficiency
  • • Big Games: Top-8 performance and knockout success
Potential Index (40% of Spurs-Fit)
  • • Age Factor: Younger managers score higher (peak at 38-42)
  • • 3-Year Trend: Performance trajectory analysis
  • • Resource Leverage: Overperformance relative to budget
  • • Temperament: Media stability and board relationships
